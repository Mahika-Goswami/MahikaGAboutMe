Career Goals

Forbes 30 under 30. CMO in a massive tech company. CEO of my own company by my 50’s. These are my career goals! Yes, they are ambitious and quite dramatic. But these are my dreams. Living in highline New York, catering to the market - or manipulating the market myself? I want to create a splash in business myself, whether that means heading marketing at a company or creating my own product. To achieve this goal, I believe it all starts with education. Post-high school, I want to major in economics and business. My dream colleges include (but are not limited to!) Stanford, UPenn, UChicago, and UC Berkeley Haas. Before my own ventures as an entrepreneur, I hope to intern at a company in my 20s to achieve experience and training in a real work setting. This is my career choice because I’ve always played the role as a consumer (I love shopping!) But what is it like on the other end? Creating the next big trend, watching as people love your product? This is what interests me the most.
